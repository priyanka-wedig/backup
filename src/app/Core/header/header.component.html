Welcome to Skeuomo Beta!
Thank you for your interest and for being a strong voice and advocate for fine arts. Your feedback will help us build the best art platform in the market. A select group of artists have been invited to test and provide feedback on Skeuomo’s pre-launch site.

What’s Involved?
Sign up, create your profile, upload your artwork and give us your feedback. It’s that simple.

What Kind of Feedback?
We want to know your personal impression, if you encountered any bugs, errors, if you find the sign up process confusing and/or if you have any recommendations on what you would like to see improved or added.

What Are The Perks?
As an early adopter you’ll receive a full year membership for free and additional renewals at 50% off for life. You’ll also have bragging rights knowing you’ve helped us build Skeuomo into a central hub for the global Art Community for years to come.

What Should Be Expected?
During this early beta phase, you will have access to a subset of features. Over the course of your testing, you may encounter the occasional broken link or error message and you’ll also find new features and the interface or user experience enhanced gradually. We have a gambit of features and robust set of tools that will enable you to do more with Skeuomo so please be patient as we work hard to finalize most features as quickly as possible.

Enjoy Skeuomo and we look forward to hearing from you.

Cesar Bohorquez Jr. Founder + CEO
Skeuomo Inc.
