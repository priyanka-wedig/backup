What is Skeuomo?

Skeuomo is the today's online art community – social network and art marketplace! We make it easier to connect, buy, sell and share artwork. Skeuomo was developed by an artist for artists. We enable artists and curators to freely express their creativity by providing an all in-one online tool to support creative your career.

We strongly believe that art isn't a predefined culture. Art is a combination of cultures, traditions, lifestyles and individual stories that makes art original and beautiful.
